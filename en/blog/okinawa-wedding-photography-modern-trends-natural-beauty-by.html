Okinawa Wedding Photography: Modern Trends & Natural Beauty by usher in making

Snap photography combined with your Okinawa trip.

Here are the Okinawa wedding photos of a Japanese couple.

These days, it's a trend to intentionally shoot panning shots with the subject slightly out of focus (pin-out),

so we utilized long exposure to create even more dynamic shots.

Wedding snaps must also evolve with the trends, right?

High-angle shots, lighting effects using natural light, and shadow scenes are also trending.

These are essential for Okinawa's summer wedding shoots, known for their intense UV rays.

Okinawa beaches aren't just about coral sand and emerald seas~

Utilizing various plants from diverse angles is

usher in making's signature emotional wedding photography, with 12 years of Okinawa snap experience.

Using Okinawa's representative plant,

the Adan tree (scientific name: Pandanus) as a backdrop for silhouette scenes~~

When we explain that the Adan tree fruit is the motif for the Gum-Gum Fruit in the One Piece anime,

everyone finds it fascinating~~
