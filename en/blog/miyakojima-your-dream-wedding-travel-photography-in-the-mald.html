Miyakojima: Your Dream Wedding & Travel Photography in the 'Maldives of the East' with usher in making

Miyakojima snap bookings confirmed for November 18th and December 2nd!

* Consultations for surrounding dates are available. Standard 1-hour shoots can also be booked.

While Okinawa snaps are our main focus and Miyakojima shoots were primarily for Japanese wedding snap clients,

the introduction of direct flights from Korea has led to frequent Miyakojima snap bookings from Korean clients.

Typically, Miyakojima snap shoots require a minimum of 2 hours of photography plus additional travel expenses from Okinawa main island.

If there are existing bookings for the above conditions, we recruit additional clients for shoots within the same period to minimize travel costs by dividing the actual expenses among them (1/n).

If the dates align, be sure to seize this opportunity!

As a Korean photographer based in Okinawa, we have secured various hidden spots throughout Miyakojima.

Experience the "Maldives of the East" with its transparent coral seas and stunning Miyako Blue – capture your memories with a snap shoot!
