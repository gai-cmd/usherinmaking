The strengths of usherinmaking

Storytelling
01

Storytelling _ A search for new and special photographs

usherinmaking never takes factory-like photos with the same location, poses, and concepts every time.

With a commitment to creating something new for every client, we discuss and propose the location and concept that best suit you.

Branding
02

Branding _ usherinmaking style, Okinawa wedding photo

Beyond beach weddings, we offer unique concepts that other photographers don't — veil scenes, night-view silhouette scenes, rainy-day scenes, and more.

Design
03

Design _ Editing skills that maximize what a photo can express

Short-looking legs, stray hair, body shape, blemishes and more —

we edit every photo with the unique usherinmaking sensibility to express its fullest beauty.

usherinmaking edits every single work by hand, with genuine care, to present it as beautifully as possible.

Consulting
04

Consulting _ Korean-wave style + a female stylist

If you want a feminine look, leave it to women.

usherinmaking is the only Korean female photographer active in Okinawa.

Dress rental service (paid); corsages, bouquets and other small items provided (free); and introductions to partner hair & makeup shops that offer Korean-style hair.
