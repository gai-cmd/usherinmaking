FAQ

What is usher in making?
It is a photo studio run by the only Korean female photographer based in Okinawa, shooting wedding, pre-wedding, self-wedding, anniversary and family photos in a soft Korean style.
What kinds of shoots do you offer?
We shoot wedding photos, pre-wedding, self-wedding, anniversary and family photos.
How much do shoots cost?
Anniversary photos start from ¥38,000 and wedding photos from ¥76,000. We offer three plans: Simple ¥76,000, Basic ¥100,000 and Afterfull ¥128,000.

We support Korean and Japanese. You can book your shoot date from the calendar on the reserve page, and reach us via the contact form, LINE or Instagram.
